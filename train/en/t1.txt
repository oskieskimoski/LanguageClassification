Spring is the most beautiful season of the year when nature awakens after a long winter. Trees begin to grow new leaves, and flowers slowly bloom across the meadows. You can hear birds singing as they return from warm countries and look for places to build their nests.

The air temperature gradually rises, days become longer, and nights become shorter. People spend more time outdoors, walking in parks or riding bicycles. Many also start working in their gardens, planting vegetables and flowers.

March can still be unpredictable – sometimes the sun shines, and a moment later it may snow or rain. However, April and May bring real spring weather, encouraging outdoor activities. Spring is a time of renewal, so many people do deep cleaning in their homes during this period.
