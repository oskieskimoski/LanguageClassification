London is one of the oldest and most beautiful cities in the world, full of historic landmarks and tourist attractions. Its history goes back over two thousand years, and it has been the capital of England for many centuries. The Tower of London, built by William the Conqueror, has served as a royal palace, prison, and treasury.

Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament are among the most recognizable symbols of London. Every hour, the bell chimes and can be heard across the city. According to tradition, the clock has remained accurate for over a hundred and fifty years.

The River Thames flows through the center of London, adding unique charm to the city, especially during summer evenings. Tourists can also visit Buckingham Palace, the official residence of the British monarch. Many people enjoy walking through Hyde Park or visiting the British Museum, which contains millions of artifacts from around the world.
