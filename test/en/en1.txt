Exercising every day is very important for staying healthy and feeling good about yourself. You do not need to go to the gym – running in the park or doing yoga at home can be just as effective. Many studies show that thirty minutes of moderate exercise five times a week is enough to keep your heart strong.

Eating well is another key to a long and happy life. Fresh vegetables, fruits, and whole grains should be the base of your diet. Try to avoid sugary drinks and processed snacks because they contain too much sugar and unhealthy fats.

Getting enough sleep is often ignored but it is just as important as diet and exercise. Adults need between seven and nine hours of sleep each night to function properly. If you have trouble falling asleep, try reading a book instead of looking at your phone before bed. Taking care of your body is the best investment you can make.
